About Us

With over 25 years of experience, WD Electrical 247 is a trusted electrical company specializing in providing comprehensive electrical services for all types of properties. Our Services

Full house rewire

A full house rewire is a comprehensive electrical project that involves replacing all the electrical wiring, switches, sockets, and fittings in a residential property. Over time, electrical systems can become outdated, unsafe, or inefficient.

Commercial refurbishments

At WD Electrical 247, we provide a full electrical service for commercial refurbishments. Whether you're renovating an office space, retail store, restaurant, or any other commercial establishment, our team of skilled electricians is ready to assist you.

Fuse board upgrades (RCD) 18th edition

Ensure the safety and efficiency of your commercial property's electrical system with our professional fuse board upgrade services. At WD Electrical 247, we specialize in upgrading outdated fuse boards to the latest 18th Edition regulations, which include the installation of Residual Current Devices (RCDs).

Extra sockets and lights

Enhance the functionality and convenience of your home or business with our professional extra sockets and lights installation service. At WD Electrical 247, we can expand your electrical system to accommodate your growing needs.

Fault finding

Experiencing electrical issues in your home or workplace? Our expert electricians at WD Electrical 247 are skilled in fault finding and can quickly diagnose and resolve electrical problems.

EV installations

As the popularity of electric vehicles (EVs) continues to rise, WD Electrical 247 is here to provide professional and reliable EV installation services. We specialize in helping homeowners and businesses transition to electric vehicles by offering efficient and safe charging solutions.
